# Best in B2B — Brand Identity

**Website:** www.bestinb2b.com  
**Created by:** H (www.hossamh.com)  
**Year:** 2025  
**Status:** Confidential and Proprietary

---

## Logo

Two versions:

- **Primary (Dark background):** White logotype on red-to-orange gradient background
- **Secondary (Light background):** Black logotype on white background

**Logotype structure:**  
"BEST IN" set in wide tracking uppercase above a large custom **B2B** wordmark where the letterforms are bold and geometric.

---

## Color Palette

Core visual identity uses a refined, earthy, and warm palette:

- **Dark Red:** `#290201`
- **Vivid Red:** `#980a0e`
- **Vivid Orange:** `#bc3111`
- **Gray Brown:** `#5d4c39`
- **Gray:** `#938b86`

Applied as full bleed backgrounds, subtle textures, and UI accents across all brand materials.

---

## Typography

**Primary Typeface:** MONA-SANS WIDE

Three weights in use:

- **Bold / All Caps** — headlines, display copy
- **Regular / Mixed Case** — body and supporting text
- **Italic / Script** — guest name titles (handwritten-style script for guest role/title)

**Character set:** Full alphanumeric + special characters  
`ABCDEFGHKLMNOPQRXYZ / abcdefghklmnopqrxyz / 123456789 !@$%^&*()_+`

---

## Brand Shapes

Geometric forms derived from the B2B lettermark:

- Large **"B" letterform** used as a frame/container for guest portrait photography
- Rounded rectangle panels used as content containers
- Concentric curved line patterns (echoing the "B" curve) used as background graphic elements
- Vertical line repetition used as a left-side texture element

---

## Graphic Elements

### Spotlight
Dramatic single-source spotlight photography (smoky atmosphere, stage lighting) used as:
- Background texture for episode covers
- Standalone atmospheric asset

### Portrait Style
All guest photography follows a consistent treatment:
- Warm amber/orange lighting with smoke/haze
- Dark moody background
- Subject in formal business attire
- Spotlight raking from upper right
- Subjects posed seated or standing with arms crossed

## Episode Cover Templates

### Template A — Full Bleed Portrait
- Guest image full frame, left-aligned text overlay
- Topic title: Bold all-caps, top-left
- Schedule info: Mid-left
- Guest name + title: Bottom-right (name bold all-caps, title in script)
- B2B logo: Bottom-left
- URL: Bottom-right corner

### Template B — B Letterform Frame
- Large "B" shape (orange circle + rounded rectangle) used as portrait cutout mask, center
- Topic title: Bold all-caps, top-left
- Guest name + title: Right of frame, same treatment as Template A
- B2B logo: Bottom-left

### Template C — Announcement (No Guest)
- Empty spotlight/stool imagery
- Large bold topic title, top-center
- Schedule info centered
- "STAY TUNED...." CTA, bottom-center

### Template D — Event/Series Announcement
- Full gradient background with concentric "B" curve lines, right side
- Bold announcement text, center
- Schedule + B2B logo standard placement
